How It Works

Experience the charm of Filipino tradition with our 100% brand new Bunot, the sustainable solution for cleaning and polishing wood and stone floors. Made from durable, dried coconut husk fibers, this eco-friendly tool brings natural brilliance to your home while supporting an earth-conscious lifestyle. Whether you're reviving hardwood or enhancing stone, Bunot offers an efficient, chemical-free way to achieve that gleaming shine.

Not just for floors, Bunot is versatile enough to be repurposed as plant pots or orchid husk, adding to your garden’s sustainability. Bring home a piece of heritage that’s as kind to the planet as it is to your floors!
